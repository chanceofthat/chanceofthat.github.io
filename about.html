About Me

Chance Daniel is a 3rd year computer engineer at Cal Poly working towards a bachelors degree of science. Over the last summer he interned at Apple in Cupertino, working as a software engineer on his own project. During his internship Chance had the opportunity to present to a senior vice president of Apple, Craig Federighi, and also earned a return offer for next summer. During the school year, Chance maintains a full course load each quarter, he also has a part time job with Cal Poly's Information Technology Services department (ITS). As a student assistant with ITS, Chance provides technical solutions for staff and students, ranging from setting up new devices to troubleshooting networks and printers. While school and work occupy most of his time, Chance also enjoys iOS Development when ever he finds a free moment. His latest endeavor is an app contracted by LA Dance Magic, that provides schedules and results for dance competitions. Apart from app development, Chance's other hobbies include hiking, volunteering, and socializing with friends.

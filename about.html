About Me

Hello! My name is Chance. I am a 3rd year computer engineering student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo working towards a bachelors degree in science. Over the summer of 2015, I interned at Apple in Cupertino, working as a software engineer on an independent project. During this incredible experience I had the opportunity to present to a senior vice president of Apple, and earned a return offer for next summer.

Throughout the academic calendar year, I maintain a full course load, meanwhile balancing extracurriculars and a part time job with Cal Poly's Information Technology Services department (ITS). As a student assistant with ITS, I provide staff and students with technical solutions, including new device setup and network / printer troubleshooting.

​While school and work occupy most of my time, I also enjoy iOS Development when ever I find myself with a free moment. My latest endeavor is multi-function app for client LA Dance Magic, that provides schedules and results of dance competitions. Apart from app development, I love to hike, give back to my community, and relax with friends and family.
